Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change my tutor?

Go to Settings (gear icon in the sidebar) and select "Change Tutor", or visit the tutor selection page directly.

How do I reset my password?

Click "Forgot Password?" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the link sent to your inbox. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.

Why isn't my math rendering properly?

Math rendering uses MathJax which loads on demand. Try refreshing the page. If the issue persists, try a different browser (Chrome or Edge recommended).

How do I switch to a different language?

Go to Settings and change your Language preference. We support 9 languages: English, Spanish, French, Arabic, Chinese, Vietnamese, Somali, Russian, and German.

What file types can I upload?

You can upload images (PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP, SVG) and PDF files. File size limits vary by plan.

How do I enable dark mode?

Toggle dark mode from the sidebar settings or the Settings page. Your preference is saved automatically.
