**Here's what happens when your kid gets a problem wrong on most math apps:**

"Incorrect. Try again."

**Here's what happens on MathMatix AI:**

The AI tutor identifies *exactly* what went wrong — was it a careless mistake? A procedural error? A gap in understanding from a previous concept?

Then it responds like a great teacher would:
